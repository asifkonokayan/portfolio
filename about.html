Who I Am

I am Riaz Ahamad, formally known as Ahamad Riaz — a 34-year-old Bangladeshi television programme producer. I have been working in private and public media since 2014, creating compelling stories for the screen.

I am a proud member of the Bangladesh Television Producers Association. Over the years, my professional experience has led me to a profound understanding of life.
